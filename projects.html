  • Astro Cat

    Astro Cat is a unique action game where you play as Major Tomcat the Astro Cat orbiting a planet. Swipe up and down to control your rocket to avoid the plentiful space junk and collect the less-plentiful fuel to stay alive. Orbit the planet for as long as possible and unlock achievements on the way. Includes a highscore leaderboard so you can compete with friends and the rest of the community.

    Check out Astro Cat on the Google Play Store.

  • Random Number Dynamo

    Random Number Dynamo is an Android application that expands the capabiltites of the traditional random number generator. Features include generating up to 100 random numbers with up to 14 decimal places and saving the generated numbers as a comma-separated value file or copying the numbers straight to your clipboard.

    Check out Random Number Dynamo on the Google Play Store.
